REASON FOR ENROLLING INTO ALTSCHOOL

In 2020, during the Covid-19 pandemic, there was a lockdown that spanned through almost a year. Schools were closed. Markets closed. Nobody could easily go to their work places nor find someone to talk to. I was locked indoors for days.

And as I watched days slip into weeks, then into months, and then for longer months, I was helpless. It was at that time I discovered that I had no digital skill. I was alone in my world. I was made to feel all the more terrible as I read that most online companies made their most income that skyrocketed their growth at the time because of the fact that human activities got another domain which is online.

Companies like google, Amazon, Youtube, Netflix and so on were looking to employ more Front end Engineers, Back-end Engineers, Cloud Practitioners and so many other skills. I though to myself that I could be one of those employed at that time if only I had the skill.

This period became for me a re-awakening to self. I got motivated to join the Tech space and contribute towards providing solutions to many of the Tech problems of the modern man. This became my major background, motivation as well as reason for enrolling into ALtschool. Thanks to Hack Sultan too, who provided me with 50% tuition scholarship that helped to ameliorate the heavy burden of paying the full tuition for me.
